Short Bio

I am a research and teaching assistant associated with the Institute for Systems Theory and Automatic Control at the University of Stuttgart. I received a B.Eng. degree in Mechanical Engineering from Deggendorf Institute of Technology and a M.Sc. degree in Engineering Cybernetics from the University of Stuttgart.
