Short Bio

Simon K. Niederländer is a Professor of Automatic Control and Engineering Mathematics at Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt. He received an M.Sc. degree in Engineering Cybernetics in 2015, and a Ph.D. degree in Systems Theory and Automatic Control in 2022, both from the University of Stuttgart. He held industrial positions as a Systems and Control Engineer at MTU Aero Engines AG from 2021 to 2023, and as a Research Scientist at Siemens AG from 2023 to 2025.
