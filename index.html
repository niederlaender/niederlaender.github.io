Short Bio

I am a research scientist with the Autonomous Systems and Control group at Siemens Technology Center Garching. I received a Dr.-Ing. degree in Systems Theory and Automatic Control and a M.Sc. degree in Engineering Cybernetics, both from the University of Stuttgart.
